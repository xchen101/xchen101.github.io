# Personal Website Design Brief
## AI UI Generation Prompt

---

## Overview
Design a minimalist personal website with street photography influences, clean grid-based layouts, and sophisticated typography. The aesthetic should feel urban, authentic, and refined without being cold or overly corporate.

---

## Visual Style

### Color Palette
- **Base**: Muted, desaturated color scheme
- **Primary colors**: Soft grays (#F5F5F5, #E8E8E8), warm off-whites (#FAFAF8)
- **Accent colors**: Desaturated earth tones (muted terracotta, dusty olive, faded denim blue)
- **Photography tones**: Natural, slightly underexposed street photography with film-like color grading
- **Text**: Charcoal gray (#2C2C2C) or deep navy (#1A1F2E) instead of pure black
- **Avoid**: Pure whites, pure blacks, saturated colors, neon tones

### Typography
- **Hierarchy-driven**: Clear distinction between headings, subheadings, body text
- **Font pairings**: 
  - Display/Headings: Modern grotesque sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica Neue, Space Grotesk)
  - Body: Geometric sans-serif or clean serif (Work Sans, IBM Plex Sans, or Crimson Text for contrast)
- **Sizes**: Large, confident headings (48-72px for hero), readable body (16-18px)
- **Weight variations**: Mix of light (300), regular (400), and bold (600-700) weights
- **Letter-spacing**: Generous tracking on uppercase headings (-0.02em on body)
- **Line-height**: Comfortable reading (1.6-1.8 for body text)

### Layout & Grid
- **Grid system**: Strict 12-column grid with clear gutters
- **Asymmetry**: Intentional off-center placements, but still grid-aligned
- **Whitespace**: Generous margins and padding (minimum 40px gutters)
- **Content blocks**: Card-based or table-like sections that respect the grid
- **Responsive**: Grid collapses cleanly on mobile (12-col → 4-col → 1-col)
- **Alignment**: Mix of left-aligned text blocks with right-aligned images/elements

### Street Photography Elements
- **Image style**: 
  - Candid, authentic urban moments
  - Architectural details (doorways, windows, shadows, textures)
  - Human elements without being overly staged
  - Natural light, golden hour tones
  - Grain/texture overlay acceptable
  - Film photography aesthetic (slight color shifts, vignetting)
  
- **Integration**: 
  - Hero images: Full-bleed or large format street scenes
  - Background textures: Subtle concrete, brick, or pavement textures at 5-10% opacity
  - Grid overlays: Thin lines that echo architectural photography grids
  - Image treatments: Subtle duotone or slight desaturation

- **Photography subjects**:
  - Urban architecture and cityscapes
  - Empty streets or minimal human presence
  - Shadows and light patterns
  - Geometric building details
  - Street-level perspectives (not aerial)

## Page-Specific Guidelines

### Homepage/About
- Hero section: Large street photography image with overlay text
- Typography lockup: Name in large display type over muted background
- Brief introduction: Short paragraph, generous line-height
- Navigation: Minimal, fixed or sticky header

### Resume Page
- **Grid-based timeline**: Use table-like structure for work history
- **Section headers**: Strong typographic treatment
- **Content blocks**: Clean separation between sections (education, experience, skills)
- **Subtle backgrounds**: Light gray blocks to separate content areas
- **Icons**: Minimal, line-based icons if used at all

### Portfolio Page
- **Image grid**: 2-3 column masonry or uniform grid
- **Project cards**: Thumbnail + title + brief description
- **Hover states**: Subtle overlay with project details
- **Detail view**: Large image + text in split-screen layout
- **Case study format**: Typography-heavy with supporting images

---

## UI Components

### Navigation
- Minimal horizontal nav or hamburger menu
- Sans-serif, uppercase, small tracking
- Hover: Subtle underline or color shift

### Buttons/CTAs
- Ghost buttons (outline only) or subtle filled buttons
- Muted accent colors, not high contrast
- Generous padding (16px vertical, 32px horizontal)

### Cards/Content Blocks
- Subtle borders or shadows (avoid heavy drop shadows)
- Muted background tints (2-5% color)
- Clear internal hierarchy

### Social Media Integration
- Minimal icon design (line icons, not filled)
- Subtle hover effects
- Integrated into footer or sidebar, not intrusive

---

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint**: Less is more—remove anything that doesn't serve a clear purpose
2. **Authenticity**: Street photography should feel genuine, not stock-photo-esque
3. **Hierarchy**: Clear visual order guides the eye
4. **Breathing room**: Generous whitespace prevents claustrophobia
5. **Consistency**: Repeat grid patterns, spacing rhythms, and typographic treatments
6. **Tactility**: Subtle textures add warmth without being decorative
7. **Sophistication**: Refined execution over trendy effects

---

## Technical Specifications

### Viewport Sizes
- Desktop: 1440px wide (max-width: 1200px content)
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Mobile: 375px - 767px

### Performance
- Optimized images: WebP format, lazy loading
- Minimal animations: Subtle fades, no flashy transitions
- Fast load times: Critical CSS inlined

### Accessibility
- WCAG AA contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body text)
- Focus states clearly visible
- Semantic HTML structure
- Alt text for all images
