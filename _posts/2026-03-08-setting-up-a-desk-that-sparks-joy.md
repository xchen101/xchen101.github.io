New keycaps, a coiled cable, and a color palette I didn't plan but somehow works. Notes on building a workspace that feels like you.

I've gone through a few desk setups over the years. The current one landed somewhere between functional and personal — a small mechanical keyboard with art keycaps, a tablet for sketching, and just enough cable management to feel intentional.

The yellow desk was a risk. Turns out it makes everything else look better.
