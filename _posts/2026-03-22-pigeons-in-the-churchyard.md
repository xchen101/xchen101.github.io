Caught a flock mid-takeoff near an old stone archway. The blur and the chaos felt more honest than anything I could have staged.

There's a churchyard I walk through most mornings. The pigeons own it. They scatter when you get close, but if you stand still long enough, they forget you're there. That morning I had the GR III set to burst — three frames before they were gone.

The motion blur was an accident, but it's the part I like most.
