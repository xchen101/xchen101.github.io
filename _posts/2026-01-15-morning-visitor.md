A crow landed on the balcony table this morning. Stayed long enough to make eye contact, then left. Urban wildlife and the small rituals of noticing.

I've been keeping track of the birds that visit the balcony. Mostly pigeons, the occasional magpie, and now this crow. It perched on the glass table, looked directly at me through the window, and stayed for maybe thirty seconds before flying off toward the next building.

There's something grounding about paying attention to the non-human neighbours.
